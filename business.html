TYPES OF BUSINESS

Regular business

If annual income is greater than 1.5 cr then you have to fill the gst form of regular business.In the regular business form there will be two kind of gst one is state gst and another is central gst.You need tp give the HSN code for each of your product.you can get the hsn codes for your product in the HSNCODE table orhere

Compensation Business

If your annual income is less than 1.5 cr then you have to fill the compensation business form.In the compensation business form 1%gst will be added to the total sale.

Others business

If you sell a product from westBengal to Bihar or any inter state then you have to fill the others business form.In the others business form you have to give Igst to each product.You have to give the HSN code for each product and their respective IGST.You can get the HSNCODE and Igst for each product here.

To pay the gst bill please choose your business type
